About Our Mission

At Green Earth Foundation, our mission is simple yet urgent: to protect our planet from the growing threat of plastic pollution. Every year, millions of tons of plastic enter our oceans, harming marine life, contaminating food chains, and endangering future generations. We believe that change begins with awareness, and awareness leads to action. Through community clean-up drives, education programs, recycling initiatives, and global campaigns, we are working to turn the tide against plastic.
